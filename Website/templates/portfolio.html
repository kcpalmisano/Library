MTA Clustering Model

This is a clustering model that is not a KNN clustering model for a client facing app. We had multiple fixed locations that we wanted to group a population by their geographic home and work. Then determine which fixed location is the closest to that particular geolocation. I then took it another step and overlaid MTA bus/train/and subway stops to show best routes. I am working on adding specialities for the various fixed locations so we can then provide information for the closest specialty location based on a clients need.
